Our customers' favourite products:

Laugenbrezel "Louise"

Louise traditionally consists of wheat flour, malt, salt, baker's yeast, fat (margarine, butter) and water. Louise takes a bath in caustic soda. Variants are available made from wholemeal, spelt or mixed flours.

Shop from $1.99

Baguette "Bernd"

Bernd is an elongated crusty white bread of French origin. The porosity of the crumb is always very coarse and uneven, the proportion of crust in relation to the crumb is high and is responsible for the strong aromatic flavor.

Shop from $3.99

Panini "Peter"

Peter is Italian-influenced pastry. A small bread (ital. pane), and comparable with the German rolls. At "Die Brezel", he comes in different shapes and sizes, and the dough is often artfully braided or looped in other shapes.

Shop from $4.99

Roggenbrot "Richard"

Richard is a real heavy-weight. Flowerwise and with respect to its size. At lbs 2.5 he feeds an entire family with tasty rye, baked to perfection in our farm-style firewood oven. "Richard" is also available in larger portions.
